It represents the most sound means of assuring the survival and advancement of our race. National Socialism was at first a political outlook adopted in several European nations, but evolved quickly into a pan-European vision.

National Socialist believe in the quest for excellence and constant improvement. National-Socialists believe the Aryan race, particularly our own people, must be the vanguard in the never-ending struggle to achieve humanity's ultimate physical and spiritual potential. Therefore, we must always ask ourselves: "is it good for our people and race?" Moral rules exist to serve the community: to ensure its long-term survival and prosperity, for the sake of its members.
